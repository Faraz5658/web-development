Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  1. 200g spaghetti
  2. 100g pancetta
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 50g grated Parmesan
  5. 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add to the pan with pancetta.
  6. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  1. 500g chicken breast, diced
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 tbsp curry powder
  5. 400ml coconut milk
  6. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and cook the onion until translucent.
  2. Add the garlic and curry powder, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh coriander before serving.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  1. 200g flour
  2. 200g sugar
  3. 75g cocoa powder
  4. 1.5 tsp baking powder
  5. 1.5 tsp baking soda
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 2 large eggs
  8. 240ml milk
  9. 120ml vegetable oil
  10. 2 tsp vanilla extract
  11. 240ml boiling water

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to cool completely before frosting.
